Visibility-Based Auto-Refresh Test

Test Instructions

This test verifies that the auto-refresh mechanism properly stops when the page is hidden and resumes when visible.

  1. Click "Start Auto-Refresh" to begin the test
  2. Watch the refresh counter increment every 2 seconds
  3. Switch to another tab or minimize the browser
  4. Wait a few seconds, then return to this tab
  5. Verify that the counter did NOT increment while the page was hidden
  6. Verify that the counter resumes incrementing after returning
